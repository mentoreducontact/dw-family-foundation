We do not sell or share your information.
And we don’t intend to.

California law requires some websites to provide a “Do Not Sell or Share” link. We provide it, not because we sell data, but because transparency is a practice. DW Family Foundation does not sell personal information for money, and does not share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

At a glance

Sale: No. We have never sold personal information for money.
Share for behavioral advertising: No. We do not allow third parties to collect personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising on our site.

Legal background — why a nonprofit has this page

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act, as amended by the CPRA (California Civil Code §§ 1798.100 et seq.), nonprofit organizations are generally exempt from CCPA obligations. Donor and supporter personal information we collect in connection with our charitable purpose is further addressed by Civil Code § 1798.105(d) and related nonprofit exemptions under California law.

In other words: the law does not currently require DW Family Foundation to provide a “Do Not Sell or Share” opt-out mechanism. We provide it anyway as a matter of best practice, because the standard we hold ourselves to is higher than the statute.

We have drafted this page to meet the technical placement and content expectations of Cal. Code Regs. tit. 11 § 7012 — clear, conspicuous link titled “Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information” in our footer, separate policy explaining what sale and sharing means, and instructions to opt out.

What “sell” and “share” mean here

For clarity:

  • “Sell” means exchanging personal information for monetary or other valuable consideration, as defined by CCPA.
  • “Share” in the CCPA sense means sharing personal information with third parties for cross-context behavioral advertising — showing you ads on other sites based on your browsing here.

What we actually do — and what we do not do

What we do not do is where trust lives, so we name it plainly:

✦We do not sell personal information for money or valuable consideration to anyone. Never have.
✦We do not share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. No ad pixel, no Meta pixel, no Google retargeting tag on our donation pages.
✦We do not allow third-party ad networks to collect data on our site for their own advertising purposes.
✦We do not upload donor emails to advertising platforms for matching, lookalikes, or custom audiences.
✦We do not track you across sites. Our analytics plan is Plausible Analytics — EU-hosted, cookie-less, no cross-site profiles, no adtech.
✦We do not broker data. We do not purchase data from brokers and do not supply data to brokers.

What we do: host a fast, static site on GitHub Pages, process donations through Stripe, reply to your emails from mentoredu.contact@gmail.com, and keep only the minimal data needed to send receipts and run programs.

If we ever changed this

We have no intention to sell or share personal information. If our model ever changed — for example, if we started using analytics that the law could interpret as “sharing” for cross-context advertising — we would: update this page, update our Privacy Policy and Cookie Policy, provide a clear, conspicuous opt-out link at the footer and in any collection point, and honor preference signals such as Global Privacy Control where feasible.

How to exercise your opt-out — even though we don’t sell

Because we already do not sell or share, there is nothing to turn off. If you want explicit confirmation that you have opted out of any future sale or sharing, you may make a request and we will record your preference.

How to request
Email us: mentoredu.contact@gmail.com
Subject line: Do Not Sell Request
Include in body: full name, email address you used on our site or donation form, and statement “I request to opt out of sale and sharing of my personal information.” If requesting on behalf of a household, list emails.
Identity verification: To protect your privacy, we will verify you by matching information you provide to our records (name + email + donation date or message thread). We may ask for a second point of verification. We will not ask for government ID, payment card numbers, or passwords.
Response time: We will confirm receipt within 10 business days and resolve your request within 15 business days, recording your opt-out in our preference log. If we need more time, we will tell you.

For requests that are not about sale/sharing — for example access, deletion, or correction — use Your Rights in our Privacy Policy or email us with subject Privacy Request.

No tracking, no advertising

We do not run ads for our own programs, and we do not allow others to run ads on our site. No pre-roll, no affiliate link, no sponsored content that tracks you. Our content is education, not commerce. Our upcoming analytics choice — Plausible — is specifically designed to avoid cookies and to not require a cookie banner in most jurisdictions.

Children

We do not knowingly collect or sell personal information of children under 13. Our Little Wonderers resources for ages 3-8 are provided to parents and educators only, with no child accounts or data collection. See Children’s Privacy.
