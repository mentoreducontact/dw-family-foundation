Accessibility

Accessibility is core to our mission. Everyday well-being support should be usable by everyone. If a barrier stops you from reading our science or joining a trail day, we want to fix it.

Our target

We aim for WCAG 2.1 AA. We use semantic HTML, sufficient color contrast, keyboard navigability, alt text for images, and clear language.

Features

  • High contrast text (ink #101010 on paper #FFFCF8, ratio > 12:1)
  • Keyboard operable navigation, no keyboard traps
  • Alt text for meaningful images, decorative images marked
  • Responsive layout that works zoomed to 200%
  • No auto-play audio or video, no flashing content
  • Readable language, limited jargon, practical steps

Feedback

If you find an issue — screen reader problem, low contrast, missing alt — email mentoredu.contact@gmail.com with subject "Accessibility — [brief description]". Include page URL, device, and assistive technology. We answer from Bay Area within 3 business days.

For event access needs (mobility, sight, hearing), tell us when you RSVP and we will plan with you.
