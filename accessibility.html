Accessibility
Belonging starts with being able to read and navigate.

DW Family Foundation is a 501(c)(3) public charity in Bay Area, CA. Our site is small, static, standards-based. We aim for WCAG 2.1 AA. If we fall short, email mentoredu.contact@gmail.com. We fix promptly.

Commitment

Everyday mental well-being affects everyone, including those using assistive technology. If our journal about movement cannot be read by screen reader, we missed point. We build semantic HTML, landmarks, heading hierarchy, alt text, keyboard operable, focus visible, 4.5:1 contrast, reflow 320-2560px, no auto-play, no flashing.

Color contrast

Paper #FFFCF8 on ink #101010 ~19:1 AAA, forest #0F1912 on cream ~18:1. Gold #C8A96A used only as accent on forest to meet AA, never body on cream.

Content

Plain English, limited jargon, transcripts if audio added later. Educational only, not medical.

Feedback

Email mentoredu.contact@gmail.com subject Accessibility feedback — page URL, issue, assistive tech used. Response 3 business days. Alternative: contact form via contact.html, or mail Bay Area, CA — email fastest.
