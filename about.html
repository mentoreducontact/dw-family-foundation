⚠️ Educational Only — Not Medical Advice

Educational purposes only, not medical advice, not therapy, not a substitute for professional care. We provide preventive mental health literacy education and practical opportunities — science-based educational resources, practical opportunities, and preventive education — not treatment, counseling, or clinical intervention. Severe cases need professional treatment. If you are in crisis: call 988 (US) or 116 123 (UK Samaritans) or go to ER.

About • Private Foundation • IRS Purpose: Education Equality • Pilot: Modern Workers

We start with modern workers who never got the manual — then expand.

We are Alan and Jacqueline — Bay Area family, first-gen and immigrant experience ourselves. DW Family Foundation is a 501(c)(3) private family foundation, 100% family-funded via business checking + brokerage. On IRS Form 1023 we filed Education Equality — because mental health literacy is uneven. Some workers get wellness stipends, therapy benefits, and managers trained in boundaries. Many first-gen professionals, immigrant tech workers, and young parents juggling work get none — just hustle content and a poster in break room. That gap is our pilot.

Pilot focus • IRS reconciliation

Who pilot serves

Primary (Fall 2026): Contemporary workers with mental sub-health lacking access — first-gen pros, immigrant tech, young parents, hourly/frontline juggling jobs — as education equality issue: they lack culturally competent, science-based mental health education + practice opportunities.

Secondary (Phase 2): Title I students needing free exam calm kits, low-income parents 3-8 needing free emotional literacy. Same free-kit + volunteer model, expanded after worker pilot playbook.

Why modern workers as education equality?

Education equality filings often conjure Title I classrooms — correct, and we will get there Phase 2. But equality also includes first-gen professionals who learned calculus, not co-regulation; immigrant workers navigating cultural stigma around stress; young parents who search at 10pm for free, evidence-based sleep + regulation, not product. Public data: over 70% report weekly stress (APA), yet culturally competent mental health literacy courses are paywalled, executive-coached, or in jargon. We provide free, science-based mental health education + practical opportunities as equity intervention — translated, trauma-aware, volunteer-practice.

Educational only. No therapy, no diagnosis. 988 for crisis.

Model: Volunteer-Led 3-5, Foundation-Supported

We provide

Free education kits (breathing cards, cortisol/rumination explainer, sleep guides, trail cards with hormone tidbits), first-aid checklist, educational waiver (not therapy), 2h training (safety, Leave No Trace, co-regulation basics, boundaries), private volunteer circle.

3-5 volunteers lead pilot

Hike Leaders: 1x/month Bay Area, 5-8mi max, tea check-in, phone-light, 6 people max (pilot small). Foundation assistant handles all research + translation internally, so volunteers focus on safety and welcoming presence, not content creation.

Why 3-5 not 10?

We are family foundation, not event org. No staff to manage 10 leaders. Small cohort = real training, real safety, real learning. Fall 2026 pilot Fall, retrospective, then expand.
