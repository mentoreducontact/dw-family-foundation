⚠️ Educational Only — Not Medical Advice

Educational purposes only, not medical advice, not therapy, not a substitute for professional care. We provide preventive mental health literacy education and practical opportunities — science-based educational resources, practical opportunities, and preventive education — not treatment, counseling, or clinical intervention. Severe cases need professional treatment. If you are in crisis: call 988 (US) or 116 123 (UK Samaritans) or go to ER.

DW Family Foundation is a 501(c)(3) private family foundation, family-funded. Our IRS filing is Education Equality. Our pilot focuses on contemporary workers struggling with mental sub-health who lack accessible, culturally competent education + practice — especially first-gen professionals, immigrant tech workers, and young parents juggling work.
