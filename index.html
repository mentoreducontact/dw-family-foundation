Not sick, not fine — human. We catch the middle early.

DW Family Foundation is a private family foundation based in the Bay Area. We focus on the quiet middle between thriving and struggling — poor sleep, constant scrolling, irritability, rumination. We work with workers, students, and young families through three steady practices: movement that regulates, trails that restore, and practical science that sticks.

01 / Move & Mood

Move to regulate

Weekly community runs, walks, and breathwork in Golden Gate Park and Presidio. We teach why 30 minutes of easy movement lowers cortisol 15-25% and raises mood chemicals — endorphin, serotonin, BDNF.

Zone 2HRVAll paces
02 / Trail Together

Trails that restore

Monthly hikes in Redwood Regional, Rancho San Antonio, and Mission Peak. Stanford research: 90-minute nature walks reduce rumination. No work talk for the first hour. Family friendly.

03 / Practical Science

Tools for daily life

For students facing exams, professionals navigating burnout, and parents raising young kids (3-8). Practical toolkits — breathing, grounding, sleep, boundaries — taught in small groups, no jargon.
