DW Family Foundation is a small, science-rooted family foundation for modern mental sub-health. We work with workers, students, and little kids (3-8) — because stress starts early and compounds quietly. We do three simple things: teach how movement regulates hormones, gather people on trails, and make emotional literacy as normal as brushing teeth.

What is 心理亚健康? Not a clinical disorder. It's the gray zone before: poor sleep, 3pm crash, endless scrolling, irritability, rumination, Sunday scaries. 73% of US adults report regular stress symptoms (APA 2023). We catch it early — with movement, nature, and language.
