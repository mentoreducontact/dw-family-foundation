Programs • 6 active • Free & low-cost • Bay Area

We practice together,
week after week.

Our work targets the middle stretch before burnout — when sleep, focus, and mood start to slide. We lead with mechanism: how movement shifts hormones, how nature restores attention, how naming feelings helps regulation. Then we practice it together.

01 / Move & Mood Lab

Move to feel steady

Weekly community run, walk, and breathwork. We teach simple physiology — cortisol, endorphins, serotonin, BDNF — and apply it in 60 minutes.

For: workers, parentsSF • Presidio & GGP
02 / Trail Together

Monthly hikes that restore

Guided Bay Area hikes with light structure. Stanford-inspired: 90-minute nature walks reduce rumination. Family friendly, tea at summit.

2nd Sat • Bay Area5-8 miles
03 / Little Wonderers

Emotional skills for young kids

For ages 3-8 and their caregivers. Play-based tools to notice, name, and practice feelings together. No screens, no pressure.

Age 3-8Play & story
04 / Classroom Calm

Exam and classroom calm

For middle school to college. Box breathing, grounding, movement breaks, sleep routine. Free kits for partner schools.

StudentsFree kits
05 / Work Well

Burnout education for work

For Bay Area teams. Burnout vs depression distinction, boundaries, meeting hygiene, manager skills for energy.

06 / Science & Stories

Clear science, human language

How we design
Who

Tech workers and parents (Bay Area 25-45), students (exam stress), young children 3-8 + caregivers. Not patients — early, everyday signals.

What

Move (regulation), Trail (restoration), Learning (skills). Small, repeatable, local.

How we know it helps

Optional pre/post check-ins: perceived stress, sleep hours, HRV trends. No medical claims. Educational only.
