⚠️ Educational Only — Not Medical Advice

Educational purposes only, not medical advice, not therapy, not a substitute for professional care. We provide preventive mental health literacy education and practical opportunities — not treatment, counseling, or clinical intervention. If you are in crisis: call 988 (US) or 116 123 (UK Samaritans) or go to ER.

We are in private pilot.

DW Family Foundation is a 501(c)(3) private family foundation, family-funded. We are currently in a private design phase. We have not finalized what events or programs we will offer. To avoid confusion, we are not listing any public programs, hikes, labs, or classes at this time.

What we ARE doing now — internal pilot only

Building volunteer capacity with 3-5 neighbors — Fall 2026

We are recruiting a pilot cohort of 3-5 volunteer Hike Leaders in the Bay Area to help us test our model: free education + practice for modern workers (first-gen, immigrant tech, young parents) who are educationally underserved in mental health literacy. This is internal, not public-facing. No public registration is open. If you are interested in the pilot design, see Volunteer pilot page.

  • No public events are scheduled or advertised.
  • We will not collect waitlists for programs that do not exist yet.
  • When we have a defined offering, we will publish it here first with clear dates, safety, and educational scope.

Why we removed the program listing

Earlier drafts listed Move & Mood Lab, Trail Together, Classroom Calm, etc. as if they were active. That was premature. We have not decided what events we will run, and we do not want someone to reach out expecting a service we cannot deliver. To respect your time and ours, we removed all program detail pages. This page will be updated only when a program is truly ready.
