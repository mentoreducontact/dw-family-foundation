Programs • 6 active • Free & low-cost • Bay Area

We do not fix people.
We practice together.

All programs target everyday sub-health — the months before burnout becomes breakdown. Mechanism first: cortisol regulation, endorphin, serotonin, dopamine, BDNF, attention restoration. Practice second: move, trail, story, community. Designed for the Bay Area and walkable in it.

01 / Move & Mood Lab

Run your feelings out — literally

Weekly community run + strength + breathwork in Golden Gate Park and Presidio. We measure HRV trends and teach hormone basics. 30 min Zone 2 lowers cortisol and boosts BDNF.

For: workers, parentsSat 8:30am • Bay Area
02 / Trail Together

Monthly hikes that quiet the mind

90-minute nature walk reduces rumination (Bratman, Stanford). No work talk first hour. Family friendly. Tea + check-in at the end. Redwood Regional, Mission Peak, Rancho San Antonio.

2nd Sat • East Bay / Peninsula5-8 miles
03 / Little Wonderers • 3-8

Kids' emotions are skills

Play-based emotional literacy for ages 3-8 through stories, games, and parent co-regulation. Naming feelings to tame them, without screens.

Age 3-8Play + stories
04 / Classroom Calm

Exam anxiety toolkit

For middle, high school, and college. Box breathing, 5-4-3-2-1 grounding, Pomodoro + movement, sleep anchor. Pilot in 3 Bay Area schools.

For studentsFree kits
05 / Work Well

Burnout vs depression — know the line

Worker-focused. Manager training, boundary setting, no-meeting mornings. WHO defines burnout as exhaustion + cynicism + inefficacy. Not a personality flaw.

Tech / ManagersWorkshop
06 / Science & Stories

We read papers so parents do not have to

Popular science hub: hormones, nature therapy, child development. Every article cites sources, gives 3 practical tips. Science, not hype.

Evidence-basedNo pseudoscience
How we design — inspired by Jed & Mind
Who

Workers (25-45), students (12-24 with exam stress), little kids (3-8 emotional literacy) in the Bay Area. Not patients — early signals.

What

Move for regulation, Trail for restoration, Story for emotional literacy. Small, repeatable, walkable.

How we know it helps

Pre/post: PHQ-2, Perceived Stress Scale short, sleep hours, self-efficacy. No medical claims. Educational only.
