⚠️ Educational Only — Not Medical Advice

Educational purposes only, not medical advice, not therapy, not a substitute for professional care. We provide preventive mental health literacy education and practical opportunities — science-based educational resources, practical opportunities, and preventive education — not treatment, counseling, or clinical intervention. Severe cases need professional treatment. If you are in crisis: call 988 (US) or 116 123 (UK Samaritans) or go to ER.

Programs • Pilot Fall 2026 • 3-5 volunteers • IRS Education Equality

Pilot: modern workers.
Phased: students & littles.

Primary pilot: contemporary workers struggling with mental sub-health who lack accessible education/resources — especially first-gen professionals, immigrant tech workers, young parents juggling work + parenting + low-wage. Framed as education equality: they didn't get culturally competent, science-based mental health education + practical opportunities. Phase 2: same free kits for Title I schools and low-income families 3-8. Volunteer-led 3-5 cohort Fall 2026 — foundation equips, volunteers lead.

Pilot logistics — why 3-5
Why workers first

Our lived experience is burnout vs meaning, high pay vs thin recovery. First-gen, immigrant, young parents face same but with less access to culturally competent education + practice. Start where we know.

Why 3-5 volunteers not 10

Family foundation, no staff. 3-5 = real 2h training (safety, Leave No Trace, co-regulation basics, waiver, boundaries), real safety checks, real debrief. Fall 2026 pilot → retro → expand.

What foundation provides

Free kits: breathing cards, grounding posters, sleep/cortisol explainer, trail cards with safety + hormone tidbit, first-aid checklist, educational waiver (not therapy), private volunteer community.
