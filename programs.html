Programs • 6 live • Free & low-cost

We don’t fix people.
We practice together.

All programs target psychological sub-health (心理亚健康) — the months before burnout. Mechanism first: cortisol, endorphin, serotonin, dopamine, BDNF, attention restoration. Practice second: move, trail, story. Inspired by Active Minds, Jed, Mind, Child Mind Institute.

01 / Move & Mood Lab • 运动与激素

Run your feelings out — literally

Weekly community run + strength + breathwork. We measure HRV, teach hormone science. 30 min Zone 2 drops cortisol 15-25%, boosts BDNF (brain fertilizer).

For: workers, parentsMon 6:30am Green Lake
02 / Trail Together • 户外联结

Monthly hikes that quiet the mind

90-min nature walk reduces rumination (Bratman Stanford). No work talk first hour. Family friendly. Tea + feelings check at summit.

2nd Sat / Seattle5-8 miles
03 / Little Wonderers • 3-8岁

Kids' emotions are skills

Emotional literacy via Daily Wonder podcast. Feelings wheel (中英双语), story pause-and-name, parent co-regulation script. Because naming is taming.

Age 3-8Podcast tie-in
04 / Classroom Calm • 学生

Exam anxiety toolkit

For middle/high school + college. Box breathing, 5-4-3-2-1 grounding, Pomodoro + movement, sleep protocol. Pilot in 3 Seattle schools.

For studentsFree kits
05 / Work Well • 打工人

Burnout vs depression — know the line

Tech-worker focused. Manager training, boundary setting, no-meeting mornings. WHO: burnout = exhaustion + cynicism + inefficacy. Not a personality flaw.

Tech / ManagersWorkshop
06 / Science & Stories • 科普

We read papers so parents don’t have to

Popular science hub: hormones, nature therapy, child development. Every article cites source, gives 3 practical tips. Chinese + English.

BilingualNo pseudoscience
