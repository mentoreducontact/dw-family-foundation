3. Purpose & mission

DWFF’s current mission: help modern people improve psychological sub-health (心理亚健康) — for workers, students, and little kids 3-8 — through movement, nature, and storytelling. Programs: Move & Mood Lab (exercise hormone science), Trail Together (nature anti-rumination), Little Wonderers (emotional literacy 3-8), Classroom Calm (exam anxiety toolkit), Work Well (burnout vs depression), Science & Stories (科普). We borrow learnings from Active Minds, Jed Foundation, Mind UK, Child Mind Institute, Bring Change 2 Mind.

This site describes programs, publishes educational journal articles, and solicits donations. Purpose evolves but transparency remains — Git history preserved.
