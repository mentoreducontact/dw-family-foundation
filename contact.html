Volunteer roles • No commitment overload • Seattle

Show up. No resume needed.

Run pacer — Move & Mood Lab

Mondays 6:30am Green Lake + Saturdays 8:30am Seward Park. Keep 9-11 min/mile conversational pace, welcome newcomers, close with breathwork. Bilingual bonus.

Trail host — Trail Together

Calm kit assembler — Classroom Calm
