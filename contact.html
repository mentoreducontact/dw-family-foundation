Volunteer roles • Bay Area

Show up. No resume needed.

We keep volunteering walkable: same park, same time, same people. No long training. You bring presence, we provide cue cards.

Run pacer — Move & Mood Lab

Saturdays 8:30AM Golden Gate Park + Presidio. Keep 9-11 min/mile conversational pace, welcome newcomers, close with breathwork.

Trail host — Trail Together

2nd Saturday, 9:30AM. Redwood Regional, Mission Peak, Rancho San Antonio. Bring thermos, first-aid kit (we provide), headcount clipboard. Family-friendly.

Calm kit assembler — Classroom Calm

Help print, cut, laminate breathing cards, 5-4-3-2-1 posters for 3 pilot Bay Area schools. Ivory paper beauty. At our kitchen table monthly.

Science translator — Science & Stories

Translate one peer-reviewed paper into 800-word human essay, plain English. Cite primary source, disclose limitations. No hype.
