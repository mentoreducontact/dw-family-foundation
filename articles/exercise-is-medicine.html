Why 30 minutes of movement lifts mood

A brisk walk looks simple but does four things: endorphins lower pain and lift mood for hours, serotonin steadies rumination, dopamine adds motivation to repeat, and BDNF supports learning and memory.

A brisk walk looks simple but does four things: endorphins lower pain and lift mood for hours, serotonin steadies rumination, dopamine adds motivation to repeat, and BDNF supports learning and memory. Studies show 30 minutes at easy talking pace, three times weekly, has an effect size comparable to some frontline options for mild to moderate low mood (JAMA Psychiatry meta-analysis 2022). The key is regularity, not intensity. Start with 20 minutes in Presidio or Golden Gate Park. Breathe through nose where possible, finish with box breathing 4-4-4-4.
