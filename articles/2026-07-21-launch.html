From idea to approved 501(c)(3):
Why we chose everyday mental well-being in the Bay Area.

We are now an approved 501(c)(3) public charity based in the Bay Area, CA. Tax-deductible donations. Here is why we picked this lane: everyday mental well-being before crisis, for workers, students, and young children.

Small is intentional

Year 1 is 100% family-funded. No gala, no merch. We run walkable things: Golden Gate Park runs, Redwood Regional hikes, classroom calm kits in three pilot schools. We publish every dollar. If it works, neighbors copy — that is scale we want.

Why not another app?

Bay Area has apps for everything. Loneliness and early stress are not fixed by another app. Belonging is built in person, in same place, same time, second Saturday. Our classroom is Presidio and Rancho San Antonio, not a screen.

Why everyday sub-health

Clinics full, prevention empty. 73% experience regular stress. Before diagnosis, years of poor sleep, irritability, doomscrolling. We teach practices with evidence: Move to regulate cortisol and boost BDNF, trail to restore attention, name feelings to tame them.

What is next

Continue free community runs, monthly hikes rotating East Bay redwoods to South Bay ridges and Presidio, expand Little Wonderers play-based sessions in libraries, provide free Classroom Calm kits. Donations tax-deductible. Email mentoredu.contact@gmail.com to volunteer, partner, or bring to your school or team.

TRY TOMORROW • BAY AREA

3 small practices

  • Walk 30 min in Golden Gate Park or Presidio — notice breath and light, no podcast.
  • One no-work-talk walk this week in Redwood Regional or Rancho San Antonio — phone on Do Not Disturb.
  • Name one feeling to one person without fixing: “I feel overwhelmed and I am going to take three breaths.”
