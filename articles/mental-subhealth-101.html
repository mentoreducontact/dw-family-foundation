You sleep six hours and wake tired. You handle work, dinner, homework — functional, but thin. Not meeting criteria for depression or anxiety, but stretched for weeks. This everyday middle zone affects many Bay Area workers, students, and parents. In US surveys, 73% report weekly stress symptoms (APA 2023).

An 8-question check: (1) Trouble falling asleep more than 3 nights/week? (2) Afternoon energy crash? (3) Irritability over small things? (4) Looping thoughts you cannot pause? (5) Endless scrolling to unwind but still tired? (6) Sunday dread? (7) Body tension in neck/shoulders? (8) Feeling you should be doing more even after doing enough? If 3+ are familiar for 2+ weeks, consider a small change: 20-min walk, 10-min early bedtime, one phone-free meal. Not diagnosis — a prompt to act early.
