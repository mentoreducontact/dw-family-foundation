Our lane • Psychological sub-health

We focus on 心理亚健康 — not clinical care. We teach how exercise regulates hormones (cortisol ↓, endorphin ↑, serotonin, BDNF), how hiking reduces rumination (attention restoration theory, Bratman 2015), how emotional literacy at 3-8 (Child Mind) prevents later crisis. Educational only, not medical advice. References cited in Journal.
