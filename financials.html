Our lane • Everyday mental well-being

Why we do not chase clinical care

We focus on everyday sub-health — not clinical care. We teach how exercise regulates hormones (cortisol down, endorphin up, serotonin steadiness, BDNF), how hiking reduces rumination (attention restoration, Bratman 2015), how emotional literacy at 3-8 prevents later escalation (Child Mind). Educational only, not medical advice. References cited in Journal.

Bay Area parks: Presidio Ecology Trail, Golden Gate Park, Redwood Regional Park Stream Trail, Mission Peak via Stanford Ave, Rancho San Antonio, Los Altos Hills — Deer Hollow Farm, Edith M. These are our classrooms.
