Donor Privacy Policy

DW Family Foundation values your trust. This donor privacy policy explains how we handle donor information.

We will not sell, share or trade our donors' names or personal information with any other entity, nor send mailings to our donors on behalf of other organizations.

That promise sits at the center of our practice. Donor information is used only for processing donations, issuing acknowledgments, tax receipts, and required regulatory filings.

What we collect

Name, contact information, donation amount and date, method of giving, DAF information if applicable, and any restriction you note.

How we use

  • Process and acknowledge gifts
  • Issue tax receipts
  • Comply with IRS recordkeeping
  • Internal reporting (aggregated, not personal)

What we do not do

  • We do not sell personal information
  • We do not share donor lists
  • We do not send mailings on behalf of other organizations
  • We do not use donor data for unrelated marketing

Anonymity

You may give anonymously. Tell us in your email and we will honor it in all public listings. Anonymous donors remain in private IRS records as required by law but not in public materials.

Access and deletion

For donor privacy questions, to request access, deletion, or anonymity: mentoredu.contact@gmail.com. Subject: Donor Privacy. We answer manually from the Bay Area, usually within 3 business days.

Security

Donation records stored with care, access limited to founders. When we enable online payments, processor security standards (e.g., Stripe) apply.
