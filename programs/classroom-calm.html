Classroom Calm
A calm classroom starts with small habits.

Pilot toolkits for middle school to college: simple, free, teacher-led.

Toolkit

Box breathing: 4-4-4-4 for 60 seconds before tests.
5-4-3-2-1 grounding: Name 5 things you see, 4 you feel, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, 1 you taste.
Movement breaks: Every 50 minutes, 5 minutes of movement — walk, squats, shoulder rolls, water.
Sleep routine: Consistent wind-down, no screens last 30 minutes, morning light.

Bay Area schools: we deliver a kit with posters and cards and a 20-minute teacher orientation. No cost.
