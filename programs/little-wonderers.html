Little Wonderers
Emotional skills for young children through play.

Early emotional skills are built through repetition with a calm adult. We support parents and caregivers of kids 3-8 with simple, play-based tools.

What we teach

Notice body signals, name the feeling, practice together. We call it Notice, Name, Do Together. Example: tight fists, 'I feel mad', push hands on wall 5 seconds then smell a flower 4 seconds. Research from Child Mind Institute and others shows naming helps regulation and builds interoception.

What a session includes

Story cards, printable feelings chart, parent script that is 30 seconds long. No lectures. We practice during play, bath time, or walks. For childcare centers, we provide a short circle-time routine.

We do not diagnose or provide therapy. We provide everyday tools that complement — not replace — professional care if needed.
