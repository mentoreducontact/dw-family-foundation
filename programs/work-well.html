WHAT • 做什么

90-min manager lab: how to spot burnout early, have a caring conversation, redesign work not just resilience. Team toolkit: meeting hygiene, no-meeting blocks, off-ramps, recovery rituals. Quarterly burnout pulse (anonymous, team-level only), office hours with facilitator.

SCIENCE • 科学

Burnout vs. depression — distinction matters (区分很关键)

Burnout is occupational phenomenon (WHO ICD-11): exhaustion, cynicism, inefficacy driven by chronic workplace stress. Depression (抑郁症) is pervasive mood disorder across domains. Overlap 30-50% but treatment differs. Confusing them delays care. We teach managers to not diagnose, but to notice and refer (不诊断，只关怀和转介).

Why tech needs its own version (为什么科技行业需要定制版)

Tech culture glorifies "hardcore" and ships guilt. On-call rotations fragment sleep. Performance review cycles create months-long chronic threat. And many brilliant coders have thin window for emotional literacy — not deficit, just training elsewhere.

We borrow from Mind's Workplace Wellbeing standards (UK) and Jed Foundation's mental health framework for workplaces, adapted for US startups: leadership buy-in explicit, language low-stigma, tools that take <5 min, and no mandatory sharing. We also respect immigrant reality: many of our tech workers are on visas where saying "I'm struggling (我很困难)" feels risky. So we frame as performance sustainability (可持续绩效), not vulnerability.

Burnout vs. depression — a manager's guide to not playing therapist

Burnout (职业倦怠)

Definition: WHO ICD-11: syndrome from chronic workplace stress not successfully managed: exhaustion (精疲力尽), mental distance / cynicism (愤世嫉俗), reduced efficacy.
Domain: work-specific initially, recovers with time off, boundaries, work redesign.
What helps: workload adjustment, autonomy, recovery, recognition, fair process.
Manager role: Can directly influence — reprioritize, clarify, protect focus time.

Depression (抑郁症)

Definition: Persistent low mood and/or anhedonia ≥2 weeks, pervasive across life, with sleep/appetite/cognition changes — clinical diagnosis needed.
Domain: everywhere, not just work; rest does not resolve.
What helps: professional evaluation, therapy, sometimes medication, social support.
Manager role: Cannot fix — notice, express care, refer to EAP/resources, accommodate.

Key: burnout can be a pathway to depression if unaddressed. Depression can be mislabeled burnout and delay care. When in doubt, refer kindly: "I've noticed changes, I'm concerned, here are confidential resources — want me to help find time?" Never say "You seem depressed (你好像抑郁了)". Say "I've noticed you seem exhausted recently, how are you (最近看你很累，怎么了)?"

Manager Lab: 90 minutes that change team health

Module 15 min — Foundations (基础): Burnout physiology: allostatic load (累积负荷), window of tolerance (耐受窗). Why high performers mask longest. Data: Gallup 2023 — manager influences 70% of engagement variance.

Module 25 min — Notice (察觉): Early signs without diagnosing: missed 1-1s, camera off, terse PR comments, hours creep but output flat, irritability. Exercise: review anonymized Slack patterns, practice noting behavior not character.

Module 30 min — Care conversation (关怀对话): Framework: Private, 30 min, no agenda: 1) Observation "I noticed X", 2) Care "I'm concerned because I value you", 3) Curiosity "What's been most draining (最消耗你的是什么)?", 4) Co-design "One thing I can adjust this sprint?". No fixing. Role-play in trios.

Module 20 min — Redesign work (重新设计工作): Six levers: workload (WIP limit), control (choose when deep work), reward (recognition specific), community (no-meeting Wed AM), fairness (on-call rotation transparent), values (why this matters). Teams pick 1 lever to experiment 2 weeks.

We give manager pocket card: 3 questions for 1-1: energy 1-5, where support needed, one boundary you kept. Not therapy — operational hygiene.

TEAM TOOLKIT • 团队工具箱 / 5-min rituals

Small habits > big offsites

1) No-Meeting blocks (无会时段): Tue/Thu 9-12 no internal meetings. Status: auto-decline allowed.
2) Closing loop ritual (收尾仪式): End day with 3 lines: done, stuck, off my plate tomorrow. Post in #standup thread, not DM at 9pm.
3) On-call recovery (值班恢复): If paged after 10pm, following morning start after 10am no questions. Manager models.
4) 1-1 energy check (能量签到): Start 1-1 with "Energy? (1 low -5 high)" then listen. Data stays private.
5) Workload WIP limit (在制品限制): Max 3 active epics per person visualized. Saying no is protecting yes.
6) Grief for shipped guilt (对延期哀悼): Sprint retro includes "What did we not do, and why it was wise?" Reduces moral injury.
7) Quarterly pulse (季度匿名脉动): 6 questions anonymous team-level only — no individual tracking. Includes burnout (MBI-2 item), belonging (归属感), recovery opportunity.
All free: Miro template + Notion page, bilingual prompts.

What we measure, what we don't

We never collect individual health data (绝不收集个人健康数据). We measure at team level only: % managers trained, % teams with no-meeting blocks protected, anonymous pulse shift 0-10. We report aggregate in Journal. No leaderboard, no performance tie. If a company wants ROI slide, we show research linking burnout to attrition cost ($20k+ per engineer) and offer referral to clinical EAP — we are not EAP.

Founders ask: won't this lower velocity? Evidence opposite: rested teams ship fewer bugs, stay longer, and have higher PR acceptance due to better review tone. Recovery is not opposite of performance — it is fuel for it (恢复是绩效的燃料).
