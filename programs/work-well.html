Work Well
Boundaries and habits that protect energy at work.

Tech work normalizes long hours and constant responsiveness. Work Well is a practical workshop for teams and managers in the Bay Area.

Bay AreaFree • Low-costEducational only

What we cover

Burnout vs depression: WHO defines burnout as exhaustion + cynicism + reduced efficacy from chronic workplace stress. Depression is broader. Knowing the line helps you act early.
Boundaries: No-meeting windows, shutdown ritual, after-hours norms.
Manager skills: Check-ins that do not require over-sharing, workload framing, recovery signals.

Format: 45-minute team session, peer-led, science-grounded, no clinical claims. Follow-up: one-page team agreement, optional second session after 4 weeks.

Seek prompt professional support if mood is low most days for more than two weeks, sleep or appetite change markedly, or safety concerns arise. In US, call or text 988.
