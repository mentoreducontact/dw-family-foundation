12 spots. 90-min science. Tea at top.

Three Bay Area trails, small group, phone-light first hour. Based on Bratman et al., PNAS 2015 — 90-min nature walk reduces rumination and sgPFC activity vs urban walk.

What to bring

1L water, layers (Presidio windy / Redwood foggy), hat/sunscreen for Rancho exposed first 0.8 mi, trail shoes for Redwood rocky sections, small daypack. Phone-light first hour — we provide intention cards + tea.
