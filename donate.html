If you share the mission, you can give.

DW Family Foundation is a 501(c)(3) private family foundation (febb97d), 100% family-funded via business checking + brokerage account. We are NOT actively soliciting public donations. Year one we meet our 5% minimum distribution ourselves. Our scope is educational: free science-based mental health literacy + practical opportunities (movement, trails, breathing) for modern workers (first-gen, immigrant tech, young parents) as education equality. Pilot cohort 3-5 volunteer hike leaders Fall 2026.

IRS disclosure for private foundations: DW Family Foundation is a 501(c)(3) private foundation, approved. No goods or services provided in exchange for donations. Tax ID available on acknowledgment letter. State disclosures: FL, NY etc here. Contact to give — ACH/Wire, DTC, DAF

1. ACH / Wire — business checking
We have business checking only (no public Stripe primary CTA yet). Email mentoredu.contact@gmail.com for routing/wire instructions. For pilot we ask small, we can do ACH even $50. We issue receipt in 24h.

2. Appreciated securities — brokerage DTC
Brokerage account can receive via DTC. Ideal for appreciated stock (20/30% AGI limit for private foundation). Email for DTC #, account #, and instructions to give your broker.

3. DAF — Donor-Advised Fund
DAF grants welcome (Fidelity, Schwab, etc). Search "DW Family Foundation" EIN available on receipt, or email for details. DAF to private foundation allowed.

4. Check
Payable to "DW Family Foundation", mailed to Bay Area address on request.

Future: Stripe may be added for small gifts, but primary today is contact-to-give due to private foundation structure.
