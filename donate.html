Why mental sub-health, not everything else?

Because 73% of US adults report stress symptoms, 34.6% in China report sub-health mood, yet clinics are full and prevention is empty. Movement drops cortisol, nature restores attention, naming feelings builds emotional literacy at 3-8. That’s our lane. Not therapy. Daily practice.
