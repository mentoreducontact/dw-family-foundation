DW Family Foundation is a 501(c)(3) private foundation based in the Bay Area, California, approved by the IRS. Donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. We are family-funded in year one — gifts expand free programs: movement sessions, trail days, and classroom calm kits.

How gifts are used

One classroom calm kit — breathing cards, posters, timer.

Trail day costs — tea, first-aid, name tags, printing.
