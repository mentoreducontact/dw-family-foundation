Why give

Because 73% of US adults report regular stress symptoms, yet clinics are full and prevention is empty. Movement helps regulate cortisol, nature restores attention, naming feelings builds emotional literacy at 3-8. That is our lane. Not therapy. Daily practice in the Bay Area — Presidio to Mission Peak.
