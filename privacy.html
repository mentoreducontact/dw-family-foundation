Privacy Policy

DW Family Foundation privacy practices for mentoreducontact.github.io/dw-family-foundation/

DW Family Foundation ("DWFF", "we", "us") is a private family foundation based in the Bay Area, California, focused on everyday mental well-being education through movement, nature, and practical science. Our website is informational and educational only.

Data minimalism

We collect as little as possible. We do not sell personal information. We do not use advertising trackers. Our site is static on GitHub Pages. If you email us, we keep your email to respond.

What we collect

  • Emails you send to mentoredu.contact@gmail.com
  • Anonymous aggregated analytics if we enable privacy-respecting analytics later (no currently)
  • Donation information via processor (Stripe) when online donations are enabled — processor terms apply

Use

To reply, to coordinate programs, to issue donation acknowledgments, to improve content.

Retention

Emails retained until you ask deletion. Donation records retained for 7 years per IRS rules.

Contact

Foundation: DW Family Foundation, Bay Area, CA
Email: mentoredu.contact@gmail.com Subject: Privacy
We respond within 30 days.
