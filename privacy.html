Privacy Policy
Minimal by design.

DW Family Foundation is a 501(c)(3) public charity based in Bay Area, CA. We collect as little as possible, use it only to run programs and send tax receipts, and we never sell your data. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices. Contact mentoredu.contact@gmail.com.

Who we are

DW Family Foundation, Bay Area, CA, 501(c)(3) public charity. Contact: mentoredu.contact@gmail.com. This site is hosted on GitHub Pages (global CDN). Analytics, if any, is privacy-first Plausible — no cookies, no cross-site tracking.

What we collect

  • We do not collect child data. Little Wonderers is for ages 3-8 but goes through parents/adults only. No child accounts.
  • Contact you provide: If you email us, we keep your email, name if provided, message content for response.
  • Donation data minimal: Name, email, last 4 digits, amount, date via Stripe. Full card number never touches our servers.
  • Site logs: GitHub Pages may collect basic server logs for security — IP, user agent — per GitHub policy.

What we do not do

We do not sell personal information for money. We do not share for cross-context behavioral advertising. No ad pixels, no Meta pixel, no Google retargeting. No data broker purchases. No prospecting. See Do Not Sell. Donor promise: We will not sell, share or trade our donors' names or personal information with any other entity, nor send mailings to our donors on behalf of other organizations.

Legal basis and retention

We process based on legitimate interest to run a charity and legal obligation to keep tax records 7 years. Email inquiries retained 2 years then deleted. Donation records 7 years per IRS. After, minimized.

Your rights

You may request access, correction, deletion subject to tax law, and opt-out of non-receipt emails. Email mentoredu.contact@gmail.com subject Privacy Request. We respond within 45 days. We honor Global Privacy Control as Do Not Sell signal per our Do Not Sell page. Nonprofits generally exempt under CCPA per §1798.105(d)(7) & §1798.145 but we honor anyway.

Children

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child provided data, contact us to delete.

Contact

Email mentoredu.contact@gmail.com, Bay Area, CA. For donor-specific: Donor Privacy. For opt-out: Do Not Sell.
